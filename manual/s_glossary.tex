\section{Glossary}
\vskip16pt
Short definitions of objects from the Survey Solutions nomenclature are listed below.\par
\vskip16pt

\statacmd{Assignment} - an electronic order and permission to take part in data collection.\par
\statacmd{Interview} - a collection of answers to the questionnaire obtained from one of the responding units.\par
\statacmd{Map} - a digital map file in a *.tpk, *.mmpk, or *.tif (Geotiff) formats.\par
\statacmd{Questionnaire} - an electronic template of the questions plan, logic of skips and validations. Questionnaires do not contain data.\par
\statacmd{User} - a combination of role, user name and password for accessing the Survey Solutions system.\par
\statacmd{Workspace} - logically isolated areas detetermining which user may see and contribute to which survey/surveys.\par
